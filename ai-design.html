AI + Design: My process

AI is baked into my process as a creative sidekick. I use tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, Figma Make, and V0 to elevate my design process, surface blind spots, and speed up the busy work. It helps me crunch data from user behaviors and past studies—whether I’m testing hypotheses, doing bottom‑up or top‑down analysis, or translating insights into design decisions. For me, AI is an exciting frontier to explore as a designer* and a powerful tool to incorporate into the experiences I create**.
