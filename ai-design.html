AI + Design: My process

AI is baked into my process as a creative sidekick—not a replacement for creativity, which will always be uniquely mine. I use tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, Figma Make, and V0 to elevate my design process, surface blind spots, and speed up the busy work. It’s a starting point that helps me reframe problems, analyze large data sets, and inspire my team to think more critically. For me, AI is an exciting frontier*—one that’s about building new habits, embracing experimentation, and slowing down to ask better questions. It’s also a powerful tool I incorporate directly into end-user experiences whenever I can**.

*I designed my website with Cursor, Github, and Figma.
